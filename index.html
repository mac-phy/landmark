About Us

Your trusted internet service provider in Nairobi & Bomet

Landmark Network Technologies is a reliable internet service provider delivering fast, stable, and affordable connectivity solutions to homes and businesses. We proudly serve customers in Embakasi, Nairobi, and Bomet County, helping individuals and organizations stay connected in today's digital world.

Our services are designed to meet the growing demand for dependable internet—whether for home entertainment, remote work, education, or business operations. With a strong local presence and customer-focused approach, we aim to bridge the digital gap by providing quality internet backed by responsive technical support.

To provide reliable, high-speed, and affordable internet solutions that empower homes and businesses, while delivering excellent customer service and innovative networking solutions within our service regions.

Our Vision
